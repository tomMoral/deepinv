class deepinv.physics.DecomposablePhysics(U=<function DecomposablePhysics.<lambda>>, U_adjoint=<function DecomposablePhysics.<lambda>>, V=<function DecomposablePhysics.<lambda>>, V_adjoint=<function DecomposablePhysics.<lambda>>, mask=1.0, **kwargs)[source]ÔÉÅ

Bases: LinearPhysics

Parent class for linear operators with SVD decomposition.

The singular value decomposition is expressed as

\[A = U\text{diag}(s)V^{\top} \in \mathbb{R}^{m\times n}\]

where \(U\in\mathbb{C}^{n\times n}\) and \(V\in\mathbb{C}^{m\times m}\) are orthonormal linear transformations and \(s\in\mathbb{R}_{+}^{n}\) are the singular values.

Parameters:

  • U (callable) ‚Äì orthonormal transformation

  • U_adjoint (callable) ‚Äì transpose of U

  • V (callable) ‚Äì orthonormal transformation

  • V_adjoint (callable) ‚Äì transpose of V

  • mask (torch.Tensor, float) ‚Äì Singular values of the transform
